PROJECT EXECUTION SUMMARY
Risk-Aware Demand Planning Web Application
(Inventory Forecasting – Agrochemical Supply Chain)
1️⃣ PROJECT OBJECTIVE

To develop a role-based web application that helps a seed company

Collect advance booking orders from distributors

Aggregate demand by crop

Compare booked quantity against forecasted production

Identify risk of

Overproduction (dead stock)

Underproduction (shortage)

Provide decision-support visibility for production planning

The system is not an automated AI decision system.
It is a data-driven decision support framework.

2️⃣ BUSINESS CONTEXT

Real-world problem identified

Agriculture is seasonal.

Distributors pre-book seeds months in advance.

Companies produce based on expected demand.

Risks

10–15% dead stock

10–15% unmet demand

No centralized structured demand analytics system.

Decision-making is largely manual or intuition-based.

This project addresses that gap.
